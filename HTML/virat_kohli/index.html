INTRODUCTION

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, is an Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport. He is known for his exceptional batting skills, aggressive playing style, and strong leadership qualities. Kohli has achieved numerous records and accolades throughout his career, solidifying his status as a cricketing legend.
Kohli hails from Delhi, India, and showed immense talent in cricket from a young age. He rose through the ranks of Indian domestic cricket and represented the Indian Under-19 team, where he captained the side to victory in the 2008 Under-19 Cricket World Cup.
He made his international debut for the Indian cricket team in August 2008, playing an ODI against Sri Lanka. Since then, he has become a vital member of the Indian team across all three formats of the game.
Kohli's playing style is characterized by his impeccable technique, tremendous stroke-making ability, and the ability to chase down challenging targets.
