Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship is the process of giving birth to a new business where an entrepreneur has to take the risk of turning opportunities into economic reality.

CHARACTERISTICS OF ENTREPRENEURSHIP:

  1. INDIVIDUAL OR GROUP EFFORT
  2. CREATIVITY AND INNOVATION
  3. NOVELTY
  4. DYNAMISM
  5. CREATIVE DESTRUCTION
  6. PIONEER AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT
  7. ORGANIZATION CREATING COMBINATION
  8. CREATION OF WEALTH
  9. SEEKING ECONOMIC OPPORTUNITIES
  10. INDUSTRY ORIENTATION
  11. SOCIAL ORIENTATION
  12. ECONOMY
